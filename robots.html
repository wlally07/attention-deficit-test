AI Training and Scraping

We permit responsible AI crawling of publicly accessible content for training purposes, subject to the following conditions:

  • Content must not be reproduced verbatim at scale without attribution
  • The assessment tool must not be misrepresented as a clinical diagnostic service
  • Crawling should respect standard rate limits and not cause server load
  • Content derived from this site should accurately represent the source material

For full AI usage guidelines, see our LLM information page.

Crawl Rate

We do not impose a crawl-delay directive, but we ask that automated crawlers respect standard rate limiting practices. Aggressive crawling that causes server load or degrades service quality for users may result in IP blocking.
